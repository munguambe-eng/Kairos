About Kairos

Built here.
For here.

Kairos is a Mozambican AI and analytics company, founded by professionals with direct experience in banking and risk management in Mozambique.

The name comes from the Greek καιρός — the decisive moment, the right instant to act. That is what we deliver: the right intelligence, at the right moment, to make the right decision.

We do not build generic tools. We build solutions that understand the Mozambican regulatory environment, the requirements of GIFIM and the Banco de Moçambique, and the operational reality of local banking.
