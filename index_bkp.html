Detection & Classification Models
Finding what
rules cannot

Some patterns are too subtle for a rulebook and too complex for a spreadsheet. We build models that detect anomalies, classify behaviour, and surface the cases that need a human decision — while filtering the noise that does not.

Transaction anomaly detection — behaviour outside a customer's normal pattern
Customer segmentation by risk profile or product behaviour
Fraud detection trained on your own transaction history
Document classification for incoming KYC material
Network analysis — identifying connected entities and relationship risk
Built on your data
Every model is trained on your bank's own historical data — not imported from a different market or a generic dataset. The model learns your portfolio, your customers, and your specific risk environment.
Validated before deployment
No model goes into production without rigorous out-of-sample testing, performance benchmarking, and documentation your risk committee can review and sign off on. We do not ship black boxes.
Monitored in production
Kairos Vigil watches every model we deploy. When accuracy drifts — because the market shifts or customer behaviour changes — your team is alerted before the model starts producing unreliable outputs.
Regulatory Framework

Two regulators.
Every report covered.

Kairos is built around the two authorities that govern compliance for every bank in Mozambique. We know their requirements, their formats, and their deadlines.

Prudential Supervision · Compliance Reporting
Central Bank of
Mozambique
Banco de Moçambique · BdM

The Central Bank sets the framework every commercial bank must operate within — capital requirements, liquidity standards, loan classification, and periodic prudential reporting. Kairos generates all required reports directly from your data, on schedule, every time.

Capital adequacy ratio under Basel III — reported monthly
Liquidity coverage ratio — monitored daily, reported on demand
Non-performing loan ratio and provision coverage analysis
Loan stage classification under international accounting standards
KYC compliance — identity verification aligned with Central Bank directives
AML/CFT Reporting · Financial Intelligence
GIFIM
Gabinete de Informação Financeira de Moçambique

GIFIM is Mozambique's Financial Intelligence Unit, responsible for receiving financial intelligence from all banks to combat money laundering and terrorist financing. Every bank must file four types of reports. Kairos Lex generates all four automatically — including structured transaction detection.

Suspicious Transaction Reports — any amount, flagged by seven detection rules
Cash Transaction Reports — single transactions from 250,000 MZN
International Fund Transfers — from 750,000 MZN per transfer
Electronic Fund Transfers — from 750,000 MZN per transfer
Cumulative structuring detection — across daily, weekly, and monthly windows

About Kairos

Built here.
For here.

Kairos is a Mozambican AI and analytics company, founded by professionals with direct experience in banking, risk management, and data in Mozambique.

The name comes from the Greek kairós — the decisive moment. Not just any moment, but the right one. That is what we deliver: the right intelligence, at the right moment, to make the right call.

We do not build generic tools and adapt them for Mozambique. We build specifically for the institutions and regulators that operate here — with a deep understanding of what the Central Bank requires, how GIFIM works, and what banks in this market actually need from their data.
