STR Detection

Seven rules.
One score.

Every transaction is evaluated against all seven rules simultaneously. The composite score drives urgency and prioritisation for the compliance team.

Rule 01
Structuring (Smurfing)
Amounts just below the CTR threshold — classic structuring pattern
+40 pts
Rule 02
High-Risk Country (FATF)
Transaction involving FATF blacklist or grey-list jurisdiction
+35 pts
Rule 03
Enhanced Monitoring Country
Countries with AML/CFT deficiencies under enhanced monitoring
+25 pts
Rule 04
Large Cash Without Justification
Cash deposit or withdrawal above threshold with no supporting notes
+30 pts
Rule 05
Round Number Amounts
Large transactions with perfectly round figures — a known indicator
+20 pts
Rule 06
Suspicious International Transfer
SWIFT transactions to flagged corridors above minimum threshold
+30 pts
Rule 07
Statistical Anomaly (z > 3)
Amount more than 3 standard deviations above the client's own history
+25 pts
